Privacy Policy

Daily Pill — Last updated April 2026

Daily Pill does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Ever.

What we store

Your daily log (whether you took your pill and at what time) and your reminder preference are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone.

What we don't do

We don't use analytics, we don't have servers, we don't create accounts, and we don't share anything with third parties. There is no backend.

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to any external service to deliver it.
